Delete Your Account

Baby Tracker

We're sorry to see you go! If you've decided to delete your Baby Tracker account, you can do so directly from within the app.

All your personal data will be permanently deleted immediately.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Baby Tracker app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (usually found in the menu or profile section)
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

⚠️ Important

Once you delete your account, all your data including baby profiles, feeding logs, diaper changes, sleep tracking, and any other records will be permanently removed from our servers. This action cannot be undone.
